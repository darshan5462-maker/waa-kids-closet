Policies & Info 📋

Last updated: April 2025

🚚 Delivery Policy

We deliver across India (PAN India shipping available). Express delivery available in Harihar & Davangere.


⚡ Harihar & Davangere: Within 2 hours (Quick Delivery)
📦 Other cities: Typically 3–7 working days


Shipping Charges: Free delivery on eligible orders. Standard shipping applicable below threshold.
Payment: COD available + Online UPI payments.
Processing: Orders processed within 24 hours. Updates via WhatsApp/SMS.

🔁 Return & Replacement Policy

Request must be raised within 48 hours of delivery. Item must be unused, unwashed, in original condition with tags intact and original packaging.


Returns applicable for: Size issues · Damaged or defective products · Wrong item received
Non-returnable: Accessories (hairbands, clips, rings — for hygiene reasons) · Items purchased during sale/clearance (unless damaged).


Return Process: Share order details via WhatsApp +91 93800 13093 with images (if damaged). Our team will verify and arrange return pickup. Replacement/refund initiated after quality check.

💸 Refund Policy

Refunds (if applicable) processed within 3–5 working days. Amount credited via original payment method or store credit.

🔒 Privacy Policy

We collect your name, contact details, and address solely to fulfill your orders. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Payments are processed securely by Razorpay — we never store card details.

📏 Sizing Information

All sizes are age-based (2Y, 3Y, 4Y etc.). If your child is between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort. All clothing is pre-washed with minimal shrinkage expected. Refer to the size guide on each product page.
